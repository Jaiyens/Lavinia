## Deferred from: code review of 1-7-reconciliation-guardrail-and-honest-coverage-state (2026-06-09)

- **Account-level one-cent tolerance does not scale with member count** [src/lib/energy/reconcile.ts deriveAccountCoverage]. The account gate compares the sum of member SA printed totals to the account printed total within a flat one cent. The inputs are integer cents (so no rounding drift is introduced here), but a real PG&E account-summary total can legitimately differ from the sum of dozens of per-SA printed totals by more than one cent (PG&E's own rounding across 50+ SAs). With real multi-SA accounts (Batth: ~57 accounts, up to dozens of SAs each), a genuinely-correct account could read `needs_review`. Whether the account tolerance should scale (e.g. +/- N cents or per-SA), or whether the account should reconcile against its own printed line items rather than the sum of SA totals, is a product/threshold decision. Settle it in Story 1.8 against the real demo account, where the true rounding behavior is observable.
